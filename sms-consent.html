SMS Opt-In & Consent – Parkly

Parkly provides SMS messaging to allow customers to request their valet-parked vehicle and receive service-related updates.

Customers opt in by providing their phone number directly to a valet attendant at the time of service. Consent is obtained before any messages are sent.

Opt-in disclosure shown to customers

“By providing your phone number, you agree to receive text messages related to your valet service from Parkly. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out.”

Use of SMS messages

  • Vehicle retrieval requests and valet service updates only
  • No marketing or promotional messages are sent

Message frequency

Messages are only sent during an active valet session.

Data collected

  • Phone number
  • Name (if provided)
  • Date and time of opt-in
  • Service location

Opt-out instructions

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from messages.

Help

Reply HELP for assistance or contact:
support@theparkly.com

How opt-in works

  1. Customer provides phone number to valet attendant
  2. Customer is informed they will receive SMS messages for valet service
  3. Messages are sent only for vehicle retrieval requests
