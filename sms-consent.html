Parkly SMS Consent Policy

Parkly is a valet ticketing app used by valet attendants to manage vehicle check-ins and customer notifications. Customers receive transactional SMS messages only.

Verbal Consent Script

Valet attendants use the following exact script when collecting customer phone numbers:

"To help you retrieve your vehicle, may we send you a text message from Parkly at this number? You will receive one message when your car is checked in and can reply READY when you are ready to leave. Message and data rates may apply. You will receive one message per visit. Reply STOP at any time to opt out."

Simulated Consent Conversation

Attendant: "May I have your phone number? Parkly will send you a one-time text confirmation for your valet ticket. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out."

Customer: "Sure, it's 215-555-1234."

Attendant: [Enters phone number into Parkly app and submits check-in]

System: Customer receives SMS: "Parkly: Your valet ticket is P-001. Vehicle: Toyota Camry. Reply READY when you'd like your car. Reply STOP to opt out."

How Consent Is Recorded

When the valet attendant submits the check-in form in the Parkly app, the system logs the date, time, phone number, and ticket number. The act of submission confirms that the attendant obtained verbal consent from the customer before entering their information.

Message Frequency

Customers receive one message per valet visit confirming their check-in. Additional messages are only sent if the customer replies READY to request their vehicle.
