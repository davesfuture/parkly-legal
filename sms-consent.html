Parkly SMS Consent

Parkly provides SMS messaging to enable valet customers to request their vehicle and receive service-related updates.

Customers opt in by providing their phone number directly to a valet attendant at the time of service. Consent is required before any messages are sent.

“By providing your phone number, you agree to receive text messages related to your valet service from Parkly. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out.”

Use of SMS

  • Vehicle retrieval requests
  • Valet service updates
  • No marketing or promotional messages

Message Frequency

Messages are only sent during an active valet session.

Data Collected

  • Phone number
  • Name (if provided)
  • Date and time of opt-in
  • Service location

Opt-Out

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe.

Help

Reply HELP or contact support@parkly.com

How It Works

  1. Customer provides phone number to valet attendant
  2. Customer is informed about SMS usage and agrees to receive messages
  3. Customer receives texts to request their vehicle
